1 Introduction

The SignatureSets package provides access to a curated and extensive compendium of RNA-based Immuno-Oncology (IO) signatures. All signatures included in this package are published in peer-reviewed literature and publicly available through trusted repositories and resources. For more details, please refer to the manuscript PMID 36055464 or explore the PredictIO web application predictio.ca to easily mine this large compendium of published signatures.

2 Curated IO RNA-Based Signatures

The SignatureSets package provides access to a curated and comprehensive compendium of 55 RNA-based IO signatures. Of these, 30 signatures (55%) are associated with sensitivity to IO therapy, while 25 signatures (45%) are linked to resistance mechanisms.

These signatures were extracted from the literature and manually annotated using GENCODE version 40 PMID: 36420896, with HUGO Gene Symbols as the primary identifiers linked to Entrez Gene IDs and Ensembl Gene IDs.
